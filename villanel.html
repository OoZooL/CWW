The Villanelle of Mediocre Embarrassment


I wanted her head on a plate

this happened 10 years ago on my 17th birthday,

but surprisingly they meant to celebrate


I was asking for a surprise party

and indeed they surprised me at bay

but, I wanted her head on a plate


It was me and her and another four at that party

eighty four long braided pony tails

surprisingly they meant to celebrate


My friends found me at a lost of clarity

She had come to my party with wavy hair

and I for my part, wanted her head on a plate


Two wooden axes I had brought from Romany

on a neck that used to house 84 embroidered braids

surprisingly though, they meant to celebrate.


I had raised the axes in my inner ceremony

a sacrifice uncalled for by my friends,

for unknown reason, I had wanted her head on a plate,

surprisingly (still) I had meant to decapitate...
